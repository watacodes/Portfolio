About Me

Thanks for visiting my website! My name is Wataru and I am a Freelance Japanese Language Tutor based in Yokohama, Japan.

I have a background in a travel industry as a Bilingual Customer Experience Specialist at a multicultural company. Also, as a Translator/Proofreader at a domestic Japanese IT company, I was responsible for translating the videos uploaded by English-speaking users to introduce English videos to the Japanese audience. Later on, My role shifted to Proofreader for the same translation team.

As of 2022, I decided to start teaching Japanese as a freelance tutor and I have been teaching worldwide students at all levels in tandem with learning Web Development!
